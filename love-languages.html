Improve your English 30 days marathon

At April 1st Valeria Lukinskaya started marathon called "Improve your English in 30 days". She offers quite unusual approach focused on short everyday exercises to keep you in good fit. Each day you should do one of these: watch tv series, have an online conversation with native speaker, read a book or write something at lang-8. Also everyday you should post something to your blog.
